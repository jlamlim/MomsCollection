STUFF YOU NEED

Ingredients Comments
4 large eggs Need not be laid by kampong chickens.
100 gram caster sugar I've reduced the amount of sugar by 1/3 from most standard cake recipes - got to watch my sugar intake.
160 gram cake flour Sift the flour. Read that cake flour preferred for light cakes vs good old plain all purpose flour. Need all the help I can for a novice "baker" like me.
1 teaspoon baking powder Sift together with the cake flour. I use the double action type, just in case my cake refuse to rise.
150 gram salted butter Thawed but not melted.Cut into small pieces for easier creaming.
1 large orange Zest and juice the orange
Of course you would also need a working oven; your baking gadgets; + a bucket full of enthusiam and finally your fingers to keep them crossed.


NOW LET'S GET BUSY

  1. Preheat oven to 180¬∞C and grease your cake tin (estimate size 25x10 cm or its equivalent).
  2. Cream butter with sugar till light and fluffy.
  3. Add eggs one by one and mix them well.
  4. Sift (second sifting) in the flour, stirring well.
  5. Stir in the orange zest and juice.
  6. Pour batter in the cake tin and bake for approximately 40 minutes. Test the cake with a skewer to ensure that cake is properly baked - skewer must come up dry.
  7. Cool cake completely before serving.
