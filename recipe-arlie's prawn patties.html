Arlie's Prawn Patty

Stuff You Need

Ingredients For The Patty Comments
15 medium sized prawns Remove shell and cut prawns into 1.5 cm cubes.
2 large yellow onions Cut each into half and slice each half into strips of 0.5 cm thick.
6 stalks of spring onions Julienn them in 5 cm length.
10 stalks of coriander Cut them into about 1 cm pieces.
few dashes of white pepper
2 teaspoons of light soya sauce
pinch of salt
oil for deep frying
Batter Comments
2 heaped tablespoons of corn flour
2 heaped tablespoons of plain flour
2 eggs
Make the Dip Comments
5 small onions Dice into 0.5 cm cubes.
3 chilli Cut into 2 cm pieces.
6 teaspoons of rice vinegar
1.5 teaspoon of lime juice
1/2 teaspoon of fine sugar

Ok Let's Start

  1. Prepare the dip - combine all the ingredients for the dip - taste it and adjust the flavour to your liking.

  2. Marinade prawns in the light soya sauce, pepper and salt. Set aside for 1/2 hour.

  3. Mix the large onions, coriander, spring onions and the marinated prawns in a large bowl. Add the batter and mix well.

  4. Form the prawn batter mix into 6 cm patties and fry them in hot oil till golden brown.

  5. Serve the patties with the dip.
