MOM's COLLECTION

A collection of Agak Agak Recipes shared by family and friends, picked from hearsay and old wives' tales and some unashamedly adapted from publications.

I am grateful to all!

Most of the recipes do not include measurements and if they do, the measurements are not scientifically exact as we temper them to taste. You also just have to "Agak-Agak" (estimate / gauge) according to your liking. You've got to be kidding right?!!! No. Sorry. So, this means that you must have had, in your lifetime, tried and successfully cooked something edible in your kitchen.

I would also like to share with you Something Borrowed, some YouTube cooking videos that I like and found useful. I hope it can be the same for you too.
