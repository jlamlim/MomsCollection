MOM's COLLECTION

A collection of Agak Agak Recipes shared by family and friends, garnished with old wives' tales and some unashamedly adapted from publications.

I am grateful to all!

Most of the recipes do not include measurements and if they do, the measurements are not pointedly exact. We usually like to tamper the measurements to suit our personal tastes and we forgot to count as we continue cooking. What this means is that you should have had successfully cooked something edible in your lifetime and be able to also "Agak-Agak" (estimate / gauge) the measurements.
